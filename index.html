Tap Reporting

Authentication Required
All services require a Tap & Barrel Group Azure AD account to log in. If you cannot access a service, please contact the IT department to request login credentials.
RDA
Restaurant Data Analysis
A comprehensive dashboard that integrates POS and QSR data to visualize and analyze data.
DCR
Daily Cash Reconciliation
A service that automatically synchronizes data from POS and Square to reconcile cash.
OCR
Open Check Report
A tool to review checks not closed by the end of a business day.
EDI
Electronic Data Interchange
A service to synchronize, parse, and save GFS EDI invoices for analysis and overcharge monitoring.
EVR
Event Reporting
A one-stop location to get receipts from all events across all locations.
MMR
Menu Mapper
A tool to scrape competitors' menu data and compare it with our offerings using a drag-and-drop interface.
MTR
Menu Tracker
A service that tracks and reports on competitors' menu changes.
RIR
Review Insights
A service that synchronizes guest reviews from Google and OpenTable and provides various metrics and analysis.
